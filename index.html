More Than A Car Wash

Mobile Vehicle Care
For Busy Barbadians

Gihon Auto Spa & Detail is a professional mobile vehicle maintenance service for people who value convenience, cleanliness, and long-term vehicle care. We help Barbadian vehicle owners protect, maintain, and enjoy their vehicles with dependable car care delivered where they are.

Our focus is consistent service, professional standards, and a detail-driven finish that keeps your vehicle looking sharp between every appointment. This is car care built around your schedule, guided by Quality. Precision. Perfection.
